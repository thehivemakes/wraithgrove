2. What We Collect

We collect only what is needed to run the game across devices, enforce the purchases you have made, and serve ads. We do not collect everything we technically could.

Collected automatically (all players)

  • Anonymous device identifier — a random ID generated locally and stored in app data. Used for: save-file persistence across sessions, ad-removal entitlement enforcement. It is not linked to your name, email, or identity.
  • Gameplay events — stage starts, stage clears, level-ups, ad views, IAP attempts. Sent to our own server only (not to any third-party analytics vendor). Used to balance difficulty and detect abuse. No third-party analytics SDK (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Adjust, AppsFlyer) is installed.
  • Ad-related identifiers — Google AdMob reads device advertising IDs (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android) to serve and personalize ads. You can limit this via OS settings (see §6 below). We do not control what AdMob collects; their privacy policy governs that data.

Collected only if you opt in

  • Email address — only if you use the Account Upgrade feature for cross-device sync. We do not ask for email just to play the game.
  • In-App Purchase records — SKU ID, price, and transaction ID for purchases you make. Required for entitlement enforcement and refund handling. Payment card numbers are never stored by us (processed by Apple, Google, or Stripe).

We restrict AdMob creative categories to block: ads that auto-redirect to other apps, ads that deep-link without a user tap, and ads that simulate system alerts. If you encounter an ad that does any of those things, report it to privacy@thehivemakes.com and we will escalate to Google.

4. What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your contacts, photos, microphone audio, or precise location.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not share gameplay data or save state with advertising partners.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs (Firebase Analytics, Adjust, AppsFlyer, etc.).
  • We do not require an account to play the game. Email is optional.
  • We do not collect payment card numbers. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple, Google, or Stripe.

5. Children Under 13 (COPPA)

Wraithgrove is rated 9+ on iOS and Teen (13+) on Google Play. We target players aged 13 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has submitted personal information to us, email privacy@thehivemakes.com with the subject line "COPPA — Data Deletion Request" and we will delete the information within 30 days.

We do not direct behavioral advertising to users we know to be under 13.

6. Your Rights

EU residents — GDPR

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restrict processing — ask us to pause processing while a dispute is resolved.
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — object to processing for direct marketing or legitimate-interests purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@thehivemakes.com with the subject "GDPR Request" and your account email or in-app device ID (visible in Settings → About). We respond within 30 days.

California residents — CCPA / CPRA

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete it, and the right to opt out of the "sale or sharing" of personal information. We do not sell your personal information. We do share device advertising IDs with Google AdMob for ad purposes, which may constitute "sharing" under the CPRA.

To opt out of ad-based sharing: email privacy@thehivemakes.com with the subject "CCPA Opt-Out," or use the OS-level controls below. We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

Opt out of ad personalization (all users)

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable for Wraithgrove. AdMob will serve non-personalized ads.
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → opt out of ads personalization. AdMob will serve non-personalized ads.

7. Access, Export, and Deletion

Email privacy@thehivemakes.com with the subject "Data Request" and either your account email (if you used Account Upgrade) or your in-app device ID. We will:

  • Within 30 days — send you a JSON export of all data we hold tied to your account or device ID.
  • Within 30 days — permanently delete it, on your confirmation. Note: deleting account data also removes IAP entitlements we track (ad removal, battle pass, etc.). Apple, Google, and Stripe retain their own records per their policies; we cannot delete those.
