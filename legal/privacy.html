2. What data we collect

Wraithgrove is built on the principle of collecting as little as possible. We collect only what is necessary to deliver the game across devices and to enforce the entitlements you have purchased.

Anonymous data — collected for every player

  • Device identifier: A randomly generated string stored in local app storage (wg_device_id). It never leaves the device except when syncing a purchased entitlement to our server. It is not linked to your real-world identity, Apple ID, Google account, or any advertising profile.
  • Game activity events: Stage starts, stage clears, stage failures, level-ups, ad views, IAP attempts, duel results, forge upgrades, and relic gains. These events include your anonymous device ID and session count. They are sent to our own server — not to any third-party analytics platform. We use them to balance gameplay and detect abuse.
  • Ad-related device signals: When you are shown an ad through the Google AdMob SDK (described in §3), AdMob collects device identifiers (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android), approximate geolocation (country level), and session metadata for the purpose of serving and measuring advertisements. This is governed by Google's privacy policy, not this one. You can limit this collection using the OS-level Limit Ad Tracking setting (described in §5).

Data tied to your identity — only if you opt in

  • Email address: Collected only if you choose Account Upgrade for cross-device save sync. We use it solely to link your save state to your account. We do not use it for marketing without separate explicit consent.
  • In-App Purchase records: When you make a purchase, we receive a transaction record from Apple, Google, or Stripe (depending on your platform). This record includes the SKU identifier, price tier, and a transaction ID. We store it on our server to enforce your entitlement (e.g., ad removal) across devices.

AdMob creative restrictions

We have configured AdMob with the following restrictions (documented in the project's AD_SDK_LOCKDOWN.md):

  • Creatives that auto-redirect to another app store or app URL without a user tap are blocked.
  • Creatives that deep-link into other apps without user consent are blocked.
  • Creatives that simulate system alerts or native UI dialogs are blocked.
  • AdMob's isExternal: false flag is set on all ad units so clicks open in the system browser, not an in-app WebView.

If you encounter an ad that violates these restrictions, please report it to [ARCHITECT FILL: privacy@ email address] with a screenshot and we will report it to Google and request removal.

5. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not collect your contacts, photo library, microphone audio, camera feed, or precise GPS location.
  • We do not embed third-party analytics SDKs such as Firebase Analytics, Adjust, AppsFlyer, Amplitude, or Mixpanel. All game-event analytics go to our own server only.
  • We do not share your save state, progress data, or purchase history with advertising partners.
  • We do not run persistent background processes, access your contacts or calendar, or track your location outside the app session.

6. Your rights

Access and export

Email [ARCHITECT FILL: privacy@ email address] with the subject line "Data Request — Export". Include either your account email (if you completed Account Upgrade) or your in-app device ID (visible in-game at Settings → About). Within 30 days we will send you a JSON file containing all data we hold tied to that identifier.

Deletion

Email [ARCHITECT FILL: privacy@ email address] with the subject line "Data Request — Delete". Include the same identifier as above. Within 30 days of confirmation we will permanently delete all records tied to your account or device ID from our servers. Note: this also deletes your entitlement records, which means purchased items (including Ad Removal) will no longer be restorable through our system. Apple, Google, and Stripe maintain their own transaction records under their own retention policies; we cannot delete those on your behalf.

Limit ad personalization

iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable Wraithgrove. AdMob will continue to serve ads but they will be context-based rather than personalized.
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization". Same effect.
Ad Removal purchase: Purchasing the ad_removal SKU eliminates all ads from the game entirely, including AdMob. This is the most complete opt-out available.

Children

Wraithgrove is rated 9+ on the Apple App Store and Teen on Google Play. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users below the age of digital consent in their jurisdiction (13 in the US under COPPA; 16 in the EU under GDPR; locally variable elsewhere). If you believe a child's data has been collected, contact us immediately for deletion.
