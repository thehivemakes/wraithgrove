# MJ_BRIEFS_BUILDING_SKINS — Alliance Shop Building Skin Cosmetics

> **For Architect-driven Sonnet + Chrome MCP session only.**
> Source: `WG.Alliance.SPEND_POOL` entries `SKIN_CANNON_GOLD` and `SKIN_WALL_JADE` in `meta-alliance.js`.
> Both skins are visible to raiders (shown during defense view).

These skins share the exact composition and camera angle as their base buildings — only palette and trim change. The MJ prompt must re-describe the full composition so the Architect's Sonnet session can generate without reading additional files.

**Shared style anchor (paste verbatim after each subject prompt):**
```
ukiyo-e woodblock top-down view, paper-texture, ink-line, washed muted color, eastern folk-horror, --ar 1:1 --v 7 --stylize 1000
```

Output paths: `images/buildings/<skin_id>.png`

---

## Skin 1 — gold_trim_cannon_battery

**Catalog key:** `SKIN_CANNON_GOLD`
**Display name:** Cannon Battery: Gold Trim
**Cost:** 400 pts
**Output:** `images/buildings/gold_trim_cannon_battery.png`

**Base composition (Cannon Battery):**
Top-down view of a fortified cannon battery platform — three dark iron cannon barrels arranged in a triangular cluster on a heavy dark-wood plank platform, thick rope coiling between the barrels, supply crates stacked at the rear corners, defensive log border around the edge.

**Skin changes from base:**
- Gold-leaf trim rings inlaid on each cannon barrel at the muzzle and midpoint
- Gold border inlay running along the platform edge planks
- Small crimson fabric banner hanging from a short post at the front-center of the platform
- Overall color temperature stays dark and iron-heavy — gold accents read against the dark base, not replacing it

**Full MJ prompt:**
```
Top-down ukiyo-e view of a cannon battery fortification platform, three dark iron cannon barrels arranged in triangle formation on a heavy dark-wood plank platform, gold-leaf trim rings on each barrel at muzzle and midpoint, gold border inlay on platform edge planks, thick coiled rope between barrels, small crimson fabric banner hanging from front center post, supply crates at rear corners, defensive log border, ukiyo-e woodblock top-down view, paper-texture, ink-line, washed muted color, eastern folk-horror, --ar 1:1 --v 7 --stylize 1000
```

## Skin 2 — jade_wall_workshop

**Catalog key:** `SKIN_WALL_JADE`
**Display name:** Wall Workshop: Jade
**Cost:** 400 pts
**Output:** `images/buildings/jade_wall_workshop.png`

**Base composition (Wall Workshop):**
Top-down view of a crafting workshop station — a wide wooden workbench running left-to-right, lumber planks stacked horizontally to one side, carpenter tools (mallet, chisels, saw) laid out on the bench surface, wood shavings scattered at the base, simple log-frame walls on three sides open at the front.

**Skin changes from base:**
- Workbench top surface lacquered jade-green
- Log-frame walls replaced with jade-tinted stone or lacquered wood in cool celadon tones
- Lumber planks take on a muted cool blue-green tint (weathered jade wood)
- Tool handles are jade-green lacquered
- Wood shavings remain pale but take a slight cool grey-green tint
- Entire palette is cool — no warm browns remain

**Full MJ prompt:**
```
Top-down ukiyo-e view of a crafting wall workshop station, jade-green lacquered workbench surface running left to right, cool celadon-toned log-frame walls on three sides, muted blue-green tint lumber planks stacked to one side, jade-handled carpenter tools mallet chisel saw laid on bench surface, pale grey-green wood shavings at base, cool collected palette no warm tones, ukiyo-e woodblock top-down view, paper-texture, ink-line, washed muted color, eastern folk-horror, --ar 1:1 --v 7 --stylize 1000
```
