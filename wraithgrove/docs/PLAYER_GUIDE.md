# PLAYER_GUIDE.md — Quick-Start Guide for New Players

**Game:** Unlimited Chaos  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Author:** W-Marketing-Launch-Pack-V2, 2026-05-08

---

Unlimited Chaos is a folk-horror survival arena. You are a walker in the grove. The grove wants to kill you. This guide covers the five things you need to know to get started — and to survive past Stage 4.

---

## §1 — Combat Basics

**How fighting works:** Your character attacks automatically. You never need to tap enemies. Your job is to move, pick up drops, and choose your upgrades when they appear. Tap anywhere on the stage canvas to move.

**The auto-attack ring:** A white cooldown ring circles your character. When the ring fills, the attack fires. Your melee weapon hits everything in range. If you have a ranged weapon equipped, the companion fires simultaneously at targets farther out.

**Level-up boon drafts:** When you collect enough XP orbs to level up, the game pauses and shows you three boon cards. Pick one. Boon options: cooldown reduction (faster attacks), bonus damage, or movement speed. Choose based on what's killing you — if enemies are reaching you, take speed; if you can kite safely, take cooldown.

**Night Mode vs Day Mode:** On the stage select screen, each stage has a Day and Night button. They are the same stage with different rules:
- **Day Mode:** Standard spawn rate, standard enemy stats. Your lantern is purely visual.
- **Night Mode:** Spawn rate ×1.6, enemy HP and damage ×1.4. Your torch meter (bottom-left HUD) drains as you fight. At torch = 0, the night overlay goes nearly opaque — you can barely see. Find campfire buildings your alliance has placed or pick up dropped torches (stumps in the environment have a 20% chance to drop one) to reset the torch instantly.

Night Mode is harder but drops more relics and gives XP and coin bonuses. Start on Day Mode. Switch to Night when you can survive Stage 1 without taking damage.

**Fever Mode:** Hit a 20-kill combo without missing a contact-damage frame. All enemies start glowing orange. Drops triple. A Fever Chest spawns on the ground. Collect it before it despawns. If your combo breaks before the 10-second fever window ends, a gray screen flash and a descending audio tone play. The Fever Chest still appears on a clean expire.

**The Light Burst / in-stage skill:** Some characters have an active skill (visible on the HUD as a cooldown button). It fires when the cooldown ring fills and you tap the button. Default: releases a burst of light that damages and briefly slows enemies in a radius. Save it for boss spawns.

## §2 — Gather, Build, Defend Loop

This is the core progression loop that powers your long-term growth:

**Gather (Hunt → drops)**

Every Hunt stage drops:
- **Coins (gold):** From enemy kills and stumps (tap the stump clusters on the stage canvas to chop them). Your core currency.
- **Craft Fragments:** Rare mid-stage drops and boss rewards. Feed them into the Forge.
- **Relics:** Boss completion rewards and craft outputs. Slot up to 6 in the Relics tab.
- **XP:** From kills and stage completion. Feeds your character level in the Ascend tab.

Tip: Stumps regenerate between stages. Build campfires in Forge to have a torch relight point in Night Mode.

**Build (Forge tab → buildings, crafting, streak)**

The Forge tab has three layers:

1. **Buildings** (left grid, 4×2) — 3 slots start unlocked (Cave, Forge station, Campfire). Five more unlock as your Gear Score rises (thresholds: 500 / 1500 / 3000 / 6000 / 10000 GS), or immediately for 200 gems each. Each building generates idle coins on a tick while you're playing.

2. **Craft** — Tap the Craft ×10 button to craft 10 relics from fragments. The rarity table shifts slightly toward higher tiers as your Forge building upgrades. First crafts will mostly produce Common relics. Keep crafting — Rare and Epic show up regularly at higher Forge levels.

3. **Daily Chest** — Claim it once per day. The reward upgrades every day up to Day 7 (guaranteed Rare relic on Day 7). Missing a day resets the streak after a 48-hour grace window.

**Defend (Ascend tab → Power)**

Your Power score is the aggregate of everything you've built:

- **Level** — in Ascend tab, spend coins to level up. Each level adds base stats.
- **Ascension tier** — once per tier threshold (7 tiers total), ascend to dramatically increase Power. Costs diamonds.
- **Equipment slots** — 3 slots (Melee / Ranged / Pet). Unlock them with coins (360 for Melee, 1100 for Ranged/Pet). Equip the weapons you've picked up in Hunt.
- **Stats** — 5 upgradable stats in Ascend (Attack, HP Max, Defense, Crit, Gather). Upgrade with coins. Gather increases how many drops fly toward you automatically.
- **Relics** — 48 relics in 5 rarities. Equip 6. Their stat bonuses stack into your Power. Rarer relics add more Power per slot.

Higher Power = surviving harder stages = reaching later bosses = dropping better relics = more Power. That's the loop.

## §3 — Alliance Basics

**How to join:** Open the Alliance tab (bottom nav, second from right). Tap JOIN and pick an alliance from the roster, or tap CREATE to start your own. You can see member count and alliance Power score before joining. You do not need an alliance to play Hunt or Tower — but you miss Alliance Boss and Alliance War rewards if you're solo.

**Alliance roles:** Three roles exist — Member, Officer, Leader.
- **Member:** Can attack in Alliance Boss and Alliance War. Can join missions.
- **Officer:** All Member permissions + can approve/reject applications + kick inactive members.
- **Leader:** All Officer permissions + can set war attackers, dissolve the alliance, promote/demote.

**Alliance Boss (co-op, runs every 4 days: 3 active + 1 rest):**

While the event is active, a boss with a shared HP pool appears in the Alliance tab. Every member can deal damage to it. NPC allies (non-player members of your alliance's NPC complement) seed about 55% of the boss HP, so the boss is killable even in small alliances.

- You deal damage by entering a boss Hunt round (entry costs energy).
- Your damage contribution is tracked as a percentage of the total pool.
- Rewards tier: Bronze (1% contribution) / Silver (5%) / Gold (15%) / Legend (30%).
- Legend-tier rewards include Legendary Relic Fragments — the most efficient way to get them outside seasonal events.
- Four bosses rotate on the cycle: Wraith Father, Frozen Crone, Samurai Lord, Void Emperor. Each has different HP scaling.

**Alliance War (weekly, Fri–Sun):**

- **Fri 22:00** — Matchmaking opens. Your alliance is matched to a similar-Power NPC alliance.
- **Sat 18:00 – Sun 18:00** — 24-hour attack window. The alliance leader (or the player, in NPC-led alliances) selects up to 4 attackers. Each attacker launches one raid.
- **Sun 18:00 – Mon 00:00** — Results phase. WIN grants all participants 200 coins + 20 Alliance Points and a 24-hour Alliance War Victors buff (+50% boss damage). LOSE grants 50 coins + 5 Alliance Points.
- A Revenge Window opens for 24 hours after a loss — tap Revenge in the War tab to launch a revenge raid against the same alliance.

**Alliance Points** accumulate over time and fund Alliance Shop purchases (see the shop icon in the Alliance tab).

## §4 — Tower Climber (Tower Gauntlet)

The Tower Gauntlet is the infinite vertical mode. It runs alongside Hunt, not instead of it.

**How to enter:** On the Hunt stage select screen, tap the GAUNTLET button (below the BATTLE button). Costs 5 Energy. You do not need to have cleared any Hunt stages first.

**How floors work:**
- Each floor is a mini-Hunt with a random enemy mix drawn from 5 enemy bands (bands unlock at Floors 1, 4, 8, 13, 18 — so early floors are only lurkers and walkers; Floor 18+ adds banshees and callers).
- A mini-boss spawns at 70% of each floor's duration. Kill it to close the floor and open the buff picker.
- At the end of each floor, pick 1 of 3 buff cards. 24 possible buff types (Common, Rare, Legendary). Getting the same buff card twice upgrades it to an enhanced variant.

**Enemy scaling per floor:** HP ×1.18, damage ×1.10, speed ×1.04 (capped at 1.6× base speed). The game is winnable at Floor 1 with zero upgrades; it becomes survival management by Floor 20 without stacking the right buffs.

**Milestone chests:** Every 5 floors, a chest spawns on the floor map. Contains coins, gems, and relic fragments. You can spend 2 gems to revive once per run if you die — the floor resets to the start of the current floor.

**Leaderboard:** Your peak floor persists to your save and submits to the leaderboard (live in Phase 4; currently shows stub data). The personal-best counter is in the Tower Gauntlet entry screen.

**Strategy for new climbers:**
1. First two floors: focus on cooldown reduction or damage boons — speed is less valuable inside the confined tower arena.
2. Stack boons of the same type — two copies of the same card upgrade it to Rare tier.
3. Energy management: 5 energy per entry, energy regenerates 1 per 10 minutes, max 30 (50 with Royal Pass). Don't dump all your energy into Tower — leave 5 for Alliance Boss events.
