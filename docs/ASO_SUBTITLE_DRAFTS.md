## Candidates

### 1. `Folk-Horror Survival Arena` — 26 chars ✓

**Reasoning:** Genre-first structure matching the comp pattern (Identity V: "1v4 Asymmetrical Battle Game"). The register modifier ("Folk-Horror") front-loads the distinctive aesthetic signal; the genre ("Survival Arena") follows, giving the App Store keyword indexer two strong targets. 26 chars leaves localization budget. Best choice if the primary audience is players who search "survival arena" and convert on visual horror register.

---

### 2. `Spirits Siege · Build & Survive` — 30 chars ✓

**Reasoning:** Names the core gameplay loop (build base → survive siege) using the horror-register noun (Spirits). Middle dot creates visual rhythm without a slash or hyphen. Both "Build" and "Survive" are high-search-volume action terms. Covers all three Klovur-visible loops (Hunt, Forge, Ascend) in eight words. Best choice for players who search "build and survive" or "tower defense."

---

### 3. `Eastern Horror. Hunt. Survive.` — 30 chars ✓

**Reasoning:** Three-word staccato rhythm designed for scroll-pause. "Eastern Horror" pins the cultural register precisely — not generic "dark fantasy," specific enough to differentiate from Western horror titles. "Hunt" and "Survive" each map to a named game tab. Best choice if the primary keyword target is the Eastern folk-horror niche (Onmyoji, Identity V adjacency).

---

### 4. `Fight Spirits. Build. Ascend.` — 29 chars ✓

**Reasoning:** Imperative verb-led ("Fight") drives urgency. Covers all three meta-progression loops: combat (Hunt), base (Forge), character (Ascend). "Spirits" is the horror-register noun. "Ascend" is both a mechanic name and an aspirational register word — doubles as keyword and tone. Best choice if the primary audience is meta-progression ARPG players who need to see character growth signaled.

---

### 5. `Dark Spirits Siege – ARPG` — 25 chars ✓

**Reasoning:** Shortest candidate. Maximum localization budget. Packs register (Dark Spirits) + mechanic (Siege) + genre abbreviation (ARPG) into 25 chars. Mirrors Punishing: Gray Raven's "Fast-paced Cyber Action RPG" approach of genre abbreviation after register. Best choice for keyword density optimization — ARPG is a high-volume App Store search term.

---

## Recommendation

**Primary:** `Folk-Horror Survival Arena` (1) — clearest genre signal, room for localization, register-forward.  
**Alternative:** `Dark Spirits Siege – ARPG` (5) — highest keyword density in fewest chars.

Architect overrides all rankings. Final call belongs with App Store Connect submission.
