# STAGE_LORE — Wraithgrove 24-Stage Flavor Text

Eastern folk-horror register. IP-clean originals.
Stages 1–3: forest_summer (calm → unease)
Stages 4–6: cold_stone (winter dread)
Stages 7–9: forest_autumn (autumn ghosts)
Stages 10–12: temple (sacred grounds)
Stages 13–15: cave (ancestor caves)
Stages 16–18: eldritch (reality tears)
Stages 19–24: ascended (post-Wraith Father, reality breaks)

---

## Stage 1 — Lantern Vigil

- **subtitle:** Lanterns lit. The dead not yet stirring.
- **lore:** First night of the Lantern Festival. The shrine-keeper failed to burn the offerings before sundown. Now the red-paper lanterns sway without wind and figures pace the treeline — hungry but still slow. Someone must keep vigil until dawn. The candles must not go out. Keep moving. They are counting on it.
- **enemy register:** lurker, red_zombie, pumpkin_lantern, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Warm summer dark — paper lanterns, pine silhouettes, a distant pagoda just visible

---

## Stage 2 — Pale Crossing

- **subtitle:** She crosses when the lanterns dim.
- **lore:** The old bridge over the black river marks the edge of the safe quarter. Families burned joss paper here for a century to appease the wandering dead. Tonight the jiangshi arrive in rows — stiff-armed, following a pale woman in white. She does not run. She does not need to. The bridge is the only way through.
- **enemy register:** lurker, sprite, red_zombie, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Warm summer dark — fog at ankle height, rice-paper lanterns casting amber on still water

---

## Stage 3 — Hollow Shrine

- **subtitle:** The bride has been waiting too long.
- **lore:** Three centuries ago a bride was abandoned at the altar on her wedding night. She burned the shrine to coal and never stopped waiting. The stone walls remember her — the altar is warm to the touch. She walks between the old pines now, and she is not alone. The hollow in the stone is shaped like a doorway. It has always been open.
- **enemy register:** lurker, sprite, walker, red_zombie, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast · boss: pale_bride
- **biome flavor:** Warm summer dark — shrine ruins, scorched altar stone, ash that smells like bridal incense

---

## Stage 4 — Frostbound Watch

- **subtitle:** The watchman stopped writing weeks ago.
- **lore:** The northern garrison post has been silent since the first snow. Dispatch riders return with horses but not themselves. The fire in the watchtower still burns — someone feeds it — but the gate log stopped mid-sentence in the third week of the cold month. Something is maintaining the post. It is not who was assigned.
- **enemy register:** walker, sprite, red_zombie, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Winter stone — blue-grey fog, frost-rimed wall stones, signal lanterns burning red

---

## Stage 5 — Glacier Mouth

- **subtitle:** It was sealed for a reason.
- **lore:** The glacier carved a mouth in the mountain face and the villagers called it the Sleeping Jaw. They packed the entrance with river stones and salt charms every solstice for eighty years. This winter the charms were gone and the stones were stacked neatly aside, as if something tidied up on its way out. Something with patience.
- **enemy register:** walker, sprite, brute_small, red_zombie, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Winter stone — glacial blue shadows, fractured ice faces, bone-white frost on every surface

---

## Stage 6 — Throat of Ice

- **subtitle:** Ice remembers every throat it fills.
- **lore:** The mountain's deepest channel — where the glacier breathes. The Frozen Crone has been here since the cold locked her in, extending her reach a little further down the mountain each winter. Shards of ice orbit her like prayer beads. Her cold touches from the inside out. The throat belongs to her now.
- **enemy register:** walker, sprite, brute_small, lurker, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast · boss: frozen_crone
- **biome flavor:** Winter stone — ice-ribbed walls, shard spirals, frozen breath made solid mid-air

---

## Stage 7 — Crimson Path

- **subtitle:** The leaves fall toward you, not down.
- **lore:** The road through the autumn forest was a funeral route for eight generations. The leaves are the color of the banners from those processions. Now the callers stand at the treeline — they do not approach, they gesture with long pale arms. What they summon walks faster. Leave the road and you will not find it again.
- **enemy register:** caller, lurker, red_zombie, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Autumn dark — maple canopies in black and rust, leaf-litter underfoot, distant funeral chimes

---

## Stage 8 — Withering Grove

- **subtitle:** The grove dies again each night.
- **lore:** The grove was sacred before the war. Burned, restored, burned again. Now it burns every night in a fire that leaves no ash and dies at dawn. The sprites move through it like sparks and the callers stand at the edge, performing something between a ritual and a demand. By morning there is no evidence it happened.
- **enemy register:** caller, lurker, sprite, red_zombie, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Autumn dark — dead stand of maples, root-light glowing faint orange, smoke with no source

---

## Stage 9 — Marrow Hollow

- **subtitle:** He made the trees remember the dead.
- **lore:** The Autumn Lord learned his name from the forest — he is what the forest becomes when it stops grieving and starts keeping score. His leaf-storms strip flesh to bone in passing and the bones remember to stand up afterward. The hollow at the grove's heart is shaped like a throne. He has been sitting in it a very long time.
- **enemy register:** caller, sprite, walker, brute_small, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast · boss: autumn_lord
- **biome flavor:** Autumn dark — hollow in the grove floor, bone-pale root network, amber air with no warmth in it

---

## Stage 10 — Shrine of Embers

- **subtitle:** The incense smoke spells someone's name.
- **lore:** The temple complex was maintained by three priest families for two hundred years. One by one the families vanished between new moon and full. The offering fires never stopped burning — brighter after each disappearance. The samurai who guard the inner gate do not respond to questions. They do not respond to anything anymore. The incense still rises.
- **enemy register:** walker, caller, red_zombie, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Temple — red-amber ember light, incense coiling through the architecture, tiled floor patterns worn smooth

---

## Stage 11 — Pagoda of Bones

- **subtitle:** Each bone belongs to someone still owed.
- **lore:** The pagoda was built on the old burial grounds to honor the ancestors. The builders used shortcuts and the bones are in the foundations. Now the structure vibrates at night with the resonance of three hundred unpaid debts. The brutes here move like muscle but are made of something older. The pagoda lists slightly toward the past.
- **enemy register:** walker, sprite, brute_small, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Temple — bone-white mortar between dark stones, reliquary chambers, lanterns that do not illuminate what they hang near

---

## Stage 12 — Vault of Names

- **subtitle:** Say no name here. It will answer.
- **lore:** The innermost chamber holds the death-register for ten thousand souls — every name recorded in the surrounding valleys for four centuries. The Temple Warden keeps it sealed. He is what happens when a guardian outlives the thing it was guarding and decides to protect it anyway. His shockwave opens the seals. They must remain shut.
- **enemy register:** caller, brute_small, walker, sprite, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast · boss: temple_warden
- **biome flavor:** Temple — obsidian vault walls, name-carved stone tablets, ringing silence between footsteps

---

## Stage 13 — Mouth of Black

- **subtitle:** The cave breathes in. You were exhaled.
- **lore:** Locals call it the Mouth of Black because it exhales once at midnight — a slow cold breath that extinguishes every lantern within hearing range. What follows is the sound of things that live in total dark navigating by sounds they were not supposed to hear. You made sounds on the way down.
- **enemy register:** lurker, sprite, walker, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Cave — near-total fog, silhouette navigation, bone-dry cold at every depth

---

## Stage 14 — Lightless Tunnel

- **subtitle:** Sound travels here. Nothing else does.
- **lore:** The tunnel was carved by miners who stopped sending reports in the third week. The rescue team found the tools laid down with care, as if the miners had stepped out on a short break. The tunnels were empty. The air is still. The sprites were already here when the miners arrived. They were waiting then too.
- **enemy register:** sprite, brute_small, lurker, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Cave — light absorbed within two meters, movement-only awareness, drip-echo from somewhere deeper

---

## Stage 15 — Cradle of Maw

- **subtitle:** She waits at the bottom to be found.
- **lore:** At the deepest point is a chamber that is warm. The warmth is the Cave Mother — she has been below the world long enough to absorb its heat. Her darkness pulse is not an attack; it is breath. Her brood are not spawns; they are memories of what she once was. The cradle is comfortable. Do not stop moving.
- **enemy register:** sprite, walker, brute_small, caller, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast · boss: cave_mother
- **biome flavor:** Cave — warm dark, wet-stone smell, a faint pulse felt through the floor

---

## Stage 16 — Veil Tear

- **subtitle:** Something reached through from outside.
- **lore:** The eldritch quarter begins at the tear — a vertical wound in the air showing nothing on the other side except more dark. The callers near it are changed: their gestures have purpose now, not habit. The sprites orbit it like moths around a flame that doesn't burn. Whatever made the wound is still nearby. It did not leave.
- **enemy register:** caller, sprite, walker, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Eldritch — sigil-etched fog, violet ground static, geometric light traces along surfaces

---

## Stage 17 — Hollow Throne

- **subtitle:** The throne was filled before you came.
- **lore:** The Hollow Throne was not built — it was carved by the force of something wanting to sit, using patience as its chisel. The Wraith Father has not arrived yet. His procession of brutes and callers is his announcement. By the time the throne is visible through the sigil-smoke, you are already inside his audience chamber.
- **enemy register:** walker, brute_small, caller, sprite, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast
- **biome flavor:** Eldritch — throne-geometry visible in sigil-smoke, deep violet haze, absence shaped like architecture

---

## Stage 18 — The Wraith Father

- **subtitle:** Every other ghost was his shadow.
- **lore:** The Wraith Father came from beyond the tear — not made by this world's dead but drawn to them, as water finds a crack. He does not haunt. He collects. The armies you have fought through are his interest made physical. He has three faces and no fear and he will use all seven minutes. Every haunting was an audition.
- **enemy register:** caller, sprite, walker, brute_small, lurker, samurai_grunt, pumpkin_lantern, jiangshi, skull_swarmer, wraith_fast · boss: wraith_father
- **biome flavor:** Eldritch — maximum sigil density, triple-phase storm, the world becoming his death-register

---

## Stage 19 — Hollow Above

- **subtitle:** Past the rift, space forgot its rules.
- **lore:** The Wraith Father is gone but the rift he left is open. The Hollow Above waits on the other side — void-purple and wrong in proportion, where the horizon bends away and the sigil drones fly in patterns too regular to be alive. The memory husks are what remains of those who entered before and did not return intact.
- **enemy register:** sigil_drone, memory_husk, caller, lurker, sprite
- **biome flavor:** Ascended — space-violet void, bone-white sigil marks, geometry that doesn't remember its own shape

---

## Stage 20 — Boneworm Sky

- **subtitle:** The sky opened and the bones fell up.
- **lore:** The Boneworm Sky earns its name — the void above writhes with pale segments too large to be what they appear to be. Memory husks split and reform and split again; the brutes below are gravity-anchor points for things trying to descend. The arena floor is the safest part of this stage by a meaningful margin. Do not look up.
- **enemy register:** sigil_drone, memory_husk, brute_small, walker, caller
- **biome flavor:** Ascended — writhing pale forms at height, gravity behaving selectively, wrong silence between sounds

---

## Stage 21 — First Sigil's Throne

- **subtitle:** Hold all three fragments. Earn nothing.
- **lore:** The Echo Throne Keeper does not protect the throne — it is the throne, shattered into three moving fragments that test whether any mind can track all three at once. All three must fall for the throne to break. This is not a guardian that defends; it is a selection mechanism. Most who enter never hold more than one.
- **enemy register:** sigil_drone, memory_husk, caller, sprite, walker · boss: echo_throne_keeper
- **biome flavor:** Ascended — throne-shard geometry, triple-form field, sigil light cutting through void

---

## Stage 22 — Echo Tear

- **subtitle:** The rift is learning to stay open.
- **lore:** Past the throne the rift has widened — it tears at itself now, cycling its own edge. The echo tear is not a wound; it is a pattern that has decided to repeat. Maximum sigil drone density, maximum memory husk spawns. No boss is needed. The rift is the threat. Moving through it is the whole test.
- **enemy register:** sigil_drone, memory_husk, sprite, walker, caller, lurker
- **biome flavor:** Ascended — rift edge visible on all sides, cycling void patterns, maximum sigil saturation

---

## Stage 23 — Memory Husk

- **subtitle:** What remains when forgetting fails.
- **lore:** This stage is named for what walks it. The memory husks are not dead — they are the residue of consciousness that could not let go when the body did. They split and reform and split and each fragment remembers only the final moment of the life it was. Dense cascades of them. The survivor is whoever the husks forget first.
- **enemy register:** memory_husk, brute_small, sigil_drone, lurker, caller, walker
- **biome flavor:** Ascended — husk-light debris field, split-echo blooms, void closing in from the edges

---

## Stage 24 — The Quiet Beyond

- **subtitle:** He is quieter now. That is the problem.
- **lore:** The Wraith Father returns as a memory of himself — diminished, patient, and more precise. The Quiet Beyond is not silence; it is the space after the last sound. He arrives without announcement. The echo of every enemy you have fought is in him now. Eight waves, eight minutes, and the abyss wearing the only face it has left.
- **enemy register:** sigil_drone, memory_husk, caller, sprite, walker, brute_small, lurker · boss: wraith_father_echo
- **biome flavor:** Ascended — total void except the boss, bleeding starlight at distance, absolute stillness between attacks
